What is Send Anywhere?

A platform agnostic file sharing service which allows users to easily share digital content peer-to-peer, in real-time, without Cloud storage. We find the optimal transfer path between devices given network conditions be it through peer-to-peer or an optimal relay server. Check out our YouTube video for more information.

Why?

We wanted to leverage peer-to-peer networking to provide individuals with the simplest, quickest, and most secure file sharing service available.

Who We are?

Estmob Inc. is a startup company based out of Seoul, South Korea and the creators behind Send Anywhere. We're just a few engineers and business people who wanted to revolutionize the way we transfer data.
